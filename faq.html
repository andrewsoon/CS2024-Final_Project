FAQ

Is there any prerequisite for the lessons?

No prior experience is required.

What if I miss the lesson? Are there any replacement lesson?

Lessons will be replaced if a valid reason is provided, i.e. MC provided, or if you let the centre know in advance that you will not be able to make it for the lesson.

What is the curriculum like?

Click here for Holiday Programme

Which programme should I send my child for?

If your child is below 12, you can go for the ‘Below 12’ programme. If your child is above 12, you can go for the ‘Above 12’ programme. If you are unsure which programme is more suited for your child, you can sign up for our free trial to see which one suits your child best.

What if the timeslot that I want is unavailable?

Our classes have a fixed number of students. If the timeslot you want is unavailable and you are unable to make it for other timeslots, drop us an email or call us at +65 6391 5834 so that we can assist you.

If I do not sign up for all 3 terms, will I still be able to learn anything?

Yes, of course, you will be able to! We teach different languages every term, and each term is independent of the other.

Am I able to choose the teacher I want?

Unfortunately, no. Our teachers have pre-assigned classes hence, if you would like to attend a specific teacher’s lesson, you will have to select the timeslot he/she is taking.

How are lessons conducted?

Lessons for the ‘Below 12’ programme are conducted physically at the centre, in groups of 4 with 1 teacher, and 1 teaching assistant. Lessons for the ‘Above 12’ programme is conducted online, in groups of 4 with 1 teacher.
