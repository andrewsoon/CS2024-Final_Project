Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any prerequisite for the lessons?

No prior experience is required. The classes are beginner-friendly as our teachers will cater the classes to suit your child’s needs.

What if I miss the lesson? Are there any replacement lesson?

Replacement lessons can be arranged. Please contact us at least 2 weeks in advance for us to make the necessary arrangements.

What if the timeslot that I want is unavailable?

Our classes have a fixed number of students. If the timeslot you want is unavailable and you are unable to make it for other timeslots, contact us so that we can assist you.

Can I opt to sign up for only one term?

Of course! The curriculum taught in each term can be learned without knowledge of the other. However, the contents in each term are often used together in the real world. Thus we encourage you to enroll for all the terms so that your child can be equipped with more skills.

Which programme should I send my child for?

You are highly recommended choosing a programme that matches your child’s age as the teaching style will differ for all programmes. Alternatively, you can sign up for our free trial to see which class would suit your child best.

How are lessons conducted?

Junior: physically at the centre, in groups of 4 with 1 teacher and 1 teaching assistant.
Teen: online through zoom, in groups of 4 with 1 teacher.
Holiday: physically at the centre, in groups of 6 with 1 teacher and 1 teaching assistant.

Am I able to choose the teacher I want?

Rest assured that all our teachers are highly qualified. If you would like to attend a specific teacher’s lesson, you can choose to select the timeslot he/she is taking.

What are the teacher’s qualifications?

While our teachers typically hold degrees related to computer science, we place more value in their skillsets and personality. All our teachers were carefully shortlisted and have underwent intensive training to reach Smart Tuition’s standards of teaching. choose to select the timeslot he/she is taking.

We are hiring and looking for candidates who have a passion for coding and working with children. Click here to apply and we will contact you for an interview session.
