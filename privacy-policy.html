Smart Tuition Privacy Policy
Personal Data
Depending on the nature or purpose of the activities/interactions with us, we may collect the following data:

  1. Identification information (e.g. Name, NRIC, contact information).
  2. Computer information (e.g.IP address, geographical location, type of browser, duration of visit, page view count).
  3. Transaction information or any in relation to the web portal (e.g. application form for class bookings).
  4. Other information (e.g. demographics, interests, photos and videos taken in class).
By providing personal data related to your dependent or child, you represent and warrant that the third party has given their consent for our collection, use and disclosure of the personal data.
Collection, Use, and Disclosure of Personal Data
We may collect, use and/or disclose your personal data for any or all of the following purposes:

  1. Perform obligations in the course of or in connection with our provision of services (e.g. enquiries, application, complaints).
  2. Provide updates on programmes and promotions.
  3. Compile usage statistics to help us understand and meet consumersâ€™ needs.
  4. Publish media materials in our website, third-party websites or other platforms for marketing purposes.
Withdrawing your Consent
The consent to collect, use or disclose your personal data will remain valid until it is being withdrawn by you in writing. You may withdraw consent by submitting a request in writing to our email at learn@smarttuition.com.sg.
Access and Correction of Personal Data
You may access and/or correct your personal data by submitting a request in writing to our email at learn@smarttuition.com.sg.
Protection of Personal Data
While transmission over the Internet or electronic storage will never be fully secured, we have introduced appropriate security measures that are subjected to constant reviews and enhancements to safeguard your personal data from unauthorised access, collection, use and/or disclosure.
Accuracy of Personal Data
To ensure your personal data is up to date, complete, and accurate, please inform us if there are any changes to your personal data in writing to our email at learn@smarttuition.com.sg.
Retention of Personal Data
Your personal data may be retained for us to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, and will cease to retain it if it is reasonable to assume that retaining your data no longer serves the purpose of collection.
Transfers of Personal Data outside of Singapore
In general, your personal data will not be transferred to countries outside of Singapore. In the event we do so, we will seek your permission before the transfer is made, and will take appropriate steps to protect your data with standards that is comparable to the PDPA.
Effect of Notice and Changes to Notice
We reserve the right to revise this privacy policy from time to time without prior notice. Your continued use of our services counts as your acknowledgement and acceptance of the changes. It is good practice to visit this page periodically to review the most updated Privacy Policy.
